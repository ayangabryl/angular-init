About Vikings

The Vikings strives to source local, sustainable and organic when possible. We work hard to source premium ingredients and we cook everything from scratch with love. Overall, these factors translate to higher menu prices, but we hope that you find value and feel a sense of comfort in knowing that we aim to get better everyday at doing what is important to us.
